Understanding Assignment Weight

If your assignments are worth 30% of your course grade, and you score 80% on assignments, they contribute 0.30 × 80 = 24 points to your final grade out of 100.

How do I find out what weight each component has?

Check your course syllabus. Most instructors list the grading breakdown: e.g. "Homework 20%, Midterm 30%, Final 50%".

What if assignments have different point values?

Enter the actual point values in Score and Max Score. The calculator will correctly weight them by their point totals.
