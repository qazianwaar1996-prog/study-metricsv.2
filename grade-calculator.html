How weighting works

Your grade is calculated by taking the sum of each (Score × Weight) and dividing it by the sum of all weights. This allows you to see your standing even if you haven't finished the course yet.

Grade = Σ(Score × Weight) / Σ(Weight)

Do weights have to total 100%?

No. If your weights total less than 100%, the calculator shows your current average for the assignments you have completed so far.

What if my score is not out of 100?

You should convert your score to a percentage first. For example, if you got 18/20, enter "90" as the score.
