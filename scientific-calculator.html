Is this calculator good for exams?

This tool is for practice and homework. For formal exams, use a permitted physical calculator. Check your institution's allowed materials list.

Does it use degrees or radians for trig?

The calculator uses degrees by default — the most common choice for academic work. Radians are available via the Rad/Deg toggle.
