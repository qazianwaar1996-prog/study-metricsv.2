Study Schedule Tips

An effective study schedule allocates more time to harder subjects and spaces sessions across the week to leverage spaced repetition — reviewing material at increasing intervals improves long-term retention by up to 50%.

How much should I study per subject?

Allocate roughly 2 hours per credit hour per week. A 4-credit difficult subject might need 8+ hours; a 2-credit review course may need only 2-3.

Should I study the same subject every day?

Spacing is more effective than massing. Studying a subject 3 times for 40 minutes is better than once for 2 hours. This tool distributes subjects across days automatically.
