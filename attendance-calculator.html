How it works

Your attendance is calculated as (Attended / Total) × 100. Most institutions require a specific minimum to remain eligible for exams.

How many classes can I skip?

If your percentage is above the requirement, we calculate the maximum number of future classes you can miss without dropping below the limit.

How do I recover my attendance?

If you're below the limit, we calculate the exact number of consecutive classes you must attend to pull your average back up.
