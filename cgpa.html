How CGPA is calculated

Your Cumulative Grade Point Average (CGPA) is the weighted mean of all your semester GPAs. The formula used is:

CGPA = Σ(Semester GPA × Semester Credits) / Σ(Total Credits)

Is CGPA different from GPA?

GPA usually refers to your performance in a single term, while CGPA represents your average across your entire degree or multiple terms.

What if I don't know my credits?

If all your semesters have the same weight, you can enter "1" for the credits in every row.
