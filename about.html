About Study Metrics

A free home for the everyday tools students actually reach for, built to be fast, honest, and free of clutter.

Why we built this

The tools students need most are scattered across dozens of cluttered websites, buried under pop-ups, forced logins, daily limits and paywalls. We wanted one calm, fast place where every tool is genuinely free, works instantly, and never asks for a login.

What we stand for

Fast

Every tool is lightweight and runs instantly in your browser.

Private

Your data stays on your device. We don't collect what we don't need.

Free

No accounts, no daily limits, no locked features.

What you'll find here

  • Global GPA Converter for 30+ countries.
  • GPA and CGPA calculators for single-term and cumulative averages.
  • Target GPA, Final Grade, Grade Calculator, Percentage, Attendance and Word Counter.

How we keep it free

Study Metrics is supported by unobtrusive advertising, which lets us keep every tool completely free. Read more in our Privacy Policy.
