How It Works

Mark subjects as Done (enter obtained marks) or Pending. The calculator finds the average marks per pending subject needed to reach your target percentage.

Formula: Required Avg = (Target Total − Obtained Sum) ÷ Number of Pending Subjects

What does the ✓ / ⏱ button do?

Toggle each subject between Done (marks locked in) and Pending (marks still to be scored). Only pending subjects factor into the required marks calculation.

Can I use different max marks per subject?

Yes — each subject row has its own Max Marks field. This handles mixed-weight subjects like 50-mark practicals and 100-mark theory papers.
