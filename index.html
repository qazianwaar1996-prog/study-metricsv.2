About Study Metrics

Built for students, by students

We built Study Metrics because we were tired of cluttered websites, pop-ups, and forced signups. We wanted a single, calm home for the calculators students actually use every day.

Every tool is free, lightweight, and private. We don't collect your data because we don't need it. Study Metrics is supported by unobtrusive advertising to keep the lights on and the tools free for everyone.
