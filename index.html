FAQ

Common questions

Is Study Metrics really free to use?

Yes. Every calculator on Study Metrics is completely free, with no signup, subscription, or usage limits. The site is supported by unobtrusive advertising, not by charging students.

Do I need to create an account?

No. None of our tools require an account or login. Open any calculator and start entering your grades — your results appear instantly.

Which countries' grading scales are supported?

The Global GPA Converter supports 30+ national and university grading scales, including percentage, letter-grade, and 4.0/5.0/10.0-point systems, converting in both directions.

Is my data stored or shared with anyone?

No. Your grades and calculations are processed entirely in your browser and are never sent to a server or stored by us.

How accurate are the calculators?

Each tool follows the standard weighting and conversion formulas most universities use, though it's worth double-checking anything official against your own institution's grading policy.

Can I use these tools on my phone?

Yes. Every calculator is built mobile-first and works the same on phones, tablets, and desktops.
