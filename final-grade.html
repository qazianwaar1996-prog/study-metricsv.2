How the math works

Your course grade is a weighted average of your current performance and your final exam. The formula to find the required final score is:

Required = [Target - Current × (1 - Weight)] / Weight

What if I need more than 100%?

If the calculator shows you need more than 100%, it means your target grade is mathematically impossible based on your current standing and the weight of the final.

Is my data saved?

Yes, your inputs are saved locally in your browser's memory so they stay there if you refresh the page.
