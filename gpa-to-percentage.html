Why convert GPA to percentage?

Many scholarship applications, job applications, and international university admissions require percentage marks. If your transcript shows a GPA, this tool gives you the equivalent percentage to include on applications.

Note that conversion formulas vary by institution. The values here follow the most widely accepted approximations for each scale.

Is the conversion exact?

No universal standard exists. Different universities use slightly different formulas. This tool uses widely accepted approximations. Always verify with your institution if the stakes are high.

How do Indian universities calculate percentage from CGPA?

Most affiliated to CBSE multiply CGPA by 9.5. Others use their own multiplier. Select "India 10-point" for the standard CBSE formula.
