How grade prediction works

Each component is weighted. Your predicted grade = Σ(Score × Weight) ÷ Σ(Entered Weights). Components with no score entered are excluded from the prediction.

What if my weights do not add up to 100%?

The calculator normalises based on entered weights. If you have 60% of your grade entered, the prediction is based on that portion. The weight note below the rows shows your current total.

Can I add ungraded future components?

Yes — leave the Score field empty. The predictor will show your standing on completed work and estimate the minimum you need on remaining work to hit target grades.
