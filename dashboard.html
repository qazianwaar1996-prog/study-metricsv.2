How the Dashboard Works

  • 1
    Data stays in your browser
    All calculations are stored in localStorage — nothing is sent to any server.
  • 2
    Calculator data auto-saves
    Every calculator saves as you type. Return to any tool and your last inputs are still there.
  • 3
    Star tools to favorite them
    Tap ☆ on any card in Quick Access to pin it to your Favorites section for fast access.
  • 4
    Clearing browser data removes everything
    If you clear site data or use a different device, calculated values will not carry over.
