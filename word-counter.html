Why track word count?

Most academic assignments come with strict length requirements. This tool allows you to monitor your progress in real-time, helping you hit your targets without exceeding word limits. Reading time is estimated at a standard academic pace of 200 words per minute.

Is my writing private?

Yes. Your text never leaves your computer. We use local browser storage to save your draft, meaning only you can see what you write.

Does it count symbols?

Characters include every letter, space, and symbol. The "No spaces" metric ignores whitespace to give you the raw content count.
