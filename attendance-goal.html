How attendance percentage works

Attendance % = (Classes Attended ÷ Total Classes Held) × 100. To hit a target by semester end, you may need to attend all remaining classes or a specific number.

What is the typical minimum attendance requirement?

Most universities require 75% attendance. Some medical and engineering programs require 85% or higher. Check your institution's policy.

Can I recover from low attendance?

Yes — if you have enough classes remaining. This calculator tells you exactly how many of those remaining classes you must attend to recover.
