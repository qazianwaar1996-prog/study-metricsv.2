About This Calculator

This basic calculator supports addition, subtraction, multiplication, division, and percentage operations. It uses standard order of operations and handles decimals accurately.

Can I use keyboard shortcuts?

Yes — type digits and operators directly from your keyboard. Press Enter or = to calculate. Backspace removes the last character. Escape clears all.
